# Post Office
How to build the Redstone Mailbox:
	Materials Needed:
		35 Obsidian
		27 Wool 
		10 Redstone Torches
		10 Stone Brick Stairs
		7 Redstone blocks
		7 Sticky Pistons
		7 Redstone comparators
		5 Redstone Lamps
		5 Slime blocks
		5 Temporary Blocks
		3 Chests
		2 Trapped Chests

Instructions:
1. Place the 5 chests total in a line, swapping between regular and trapped.
2. go behind the chest and dig one down, replace with wool, then place comparators.
3. Place wool above and next to the comparators.
4. Go next to the wool next to comps and dig down, replacing with wool, then place torches on the wool touching comps.
5. place wool across from torches.
6. place obsidian above torches, then torches on obi, above wool.
7. above the torches, place sticky pistons, 1 facing the chests, then one up, and repeat till you have 3 facing the chest then 2 up.
8. Place obi on the back sides (the Right side, for those facing up) of the pistons.
9. for the 2 facing up, place Redstone blocks, then wool closer to the chests.
10. place comparators facing the Redstone blocks on the wool, then on the opposite side, place sticky pistons.
11. Place a slime block, the under it a Redstone block
12. Now above the chests, place a row of Stone Brick Stairs, then above that the lamps, then place Temporary blocks above the lamps.
13. Place another row of stairs connected to the lamps, but facing the same direction of the chests, and upside down.
14. BREAK THE TEMPORARY BLOCKS, OTHERWISE BUILD WILL NOT WORK
15. Now to the 3 neglected sticky pistons, place a slime block and Redstone Block, both linear and regular.
16. Place a 5x5 wall on the Build's right side so the window doesn't show the machinery.
17. Done.
